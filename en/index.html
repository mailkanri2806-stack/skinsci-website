ABOUT

Skincare, but with the receipts

SKINSCI is a Japanese-language skincare media built on peer-reviewed research. Our editorial principle is simple: every ingredient claim links back to published clinical studies — RCTs, cohort studies, in-vitro mechanism papers.

We serve two audiences in the same place:

  • Beginners who have skin concerns but don't know where to start
  • Advanced users who know ingredient names but want to understand why they work

Our flagship content is in Japanese. English translations of key ingredient guides are coming soon.
